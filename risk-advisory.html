Risk Advisory

We offer comprehensive advisory services to support clients in achieving their strategic objectives. These include business process re-engineering, process mapping, and control adequacy assessments. We perform enterprise-wide risk assessments and support clients to identify and manage strategic and operational risks to ensure compliance with applicable standards, laws, and regulations. Identify a complete set of IT risks and design IT governance and risk management processes to better manage them.

We perform risk and control maturity assessments on the first and second line of defense functions as well as control self-assessments to evaluate the adequacy of controls within organizations, map key business processes and work with clients to develop policies and procedures.

Our service offering includes:

  • Business Process Re-engineering
  • Control Self-Assessments
  • Control Environment Assessments
  • Combined Assurance Implementation
  • Process Mapping
  • Control Adequacy Assessments
  • IT Risk Assessments
